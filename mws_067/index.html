Episode 067: Andrew Barnett

In defense of Colin ‘Funky’ Miller

Live from EDGE HQ it’s a full house as host Mark Williamson is joined by guest Andrew Barnett as well as Lester Diamond, Ryan ‘Special Comments’ Crawford and our new Intern Jasmine Langdon.

This week we chat to Andrew about his recent trip to Tasmania, his upcoming Adelaide Fringe Festival show and we also discuss Cricket with a lot of discussion revolving around Colin ‘Funky’ Miller.
